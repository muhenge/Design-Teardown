PERSON OF THE WEEK
Tammy Everts (@tameverts) is Chief Experience Officer at SpeedCurve, where she helps companies understand how visitors use their websites. Tammy has spent the past two decades studying how people use the web and, since 2009, she’s focused on the intersection between web performance, user experience, and business metrics. Her book, Time Is Money: The Business Value of Web Performance, is a distillation of much of this research.
